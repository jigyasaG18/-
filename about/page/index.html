About Misarticulation App

Empowering kids to express themselves better!

Our Mission

Our mission is to help children overcome speech difficulties in a fun, engaging, and interactive way. Through simple exercises and supportive feedback, we hope to make speech therapy accessible and enjoyable for kids.

Project Objectives

  • Provide a platform for children to practice speech sounds effectively.
  • Use engaging visuals and audio to maintain children's interest.
  • Offer supportive, progress-tracking features for both kids and parents.

Key Features

Practice Worksheets

Interactive worksheets tailored to each child’s needs, helping them practice phonics and pronunciation.

Parent Resources

Resources and guides for parents to support their child’s progress and understand articulation development.

Zoom & YouTube Links

Access to virtual sessions and video resources to support interactive learning and practice.

Phonics Practice

Fun phonics exercises to strengthen letter-sound recognition and pronunciation.
